Attractions

Tokyo Disneyland

It was opened in 1983 as the first Disney theme park outside of the United States. Modeled after Disneyland in California and the Magic Kingdom in Florida, Tokyo Disneyland is made up of seven themed lands and features seasonal decorations and parades. This place is for all ages and prices range from 41 to 82 dollars

Sensoji Temple

The Sensoji Temple is an ancient Buddhist temple located in Asakusa, Tokyo, Japan. It is Tokyo's oldest temple, and one of its most significant.This temple is dedicated to Kannon, the Buddhist goddess of mercy, and it is incredibly popular with millions of people visiting every year. This place is for all ages.

Shibuya Crossing

Shibuya Crossing is like a giant beating heart, sending people in all directions with every pulsing light change. Nowhere else says ‘Welcome to Tokyo’ better than this. Hundreds of people and at peak times upwards of 3000 people cross at a time, coming from all directions at once, yet still to dodging each other with a practised, nonchalant agility.

Visit Site

Mario Kart

Originally named MariCar, Street Kart led tours of the city for visitors dressed in Mario, Luigi and Yoshi onesies, letting them drive go-karts on the city streets just like in the video games to see Tokyo's most famous landmarks, including Tokyo Tower, Shibuya and Akihabara. Prices start at $57 per person
